Nashville Women’s STEM group meet up

Hi everyone,

Our next meet up will be Saturday, January 19th 2:00pm at Barista Parlor 519 Gallatin Ave, Nashville, TN.

Leadership Series

This month we’ll begin our leadership discussion series around posts by, my personal hero and mentor, Kate Matsudaira. Our first discussion will be on Overcoming Self Doubt.

Feel free to read ahead, or come early and read at the Barista Parlor with me. I’ll also have printed versions available.

Looking forward to seeing you there!

Nobody is born with the skills to be a master programmer or CEO. It’s learned. And taking a risk, doing something beyond your normal experience, is what yields the greatest results. The hardest projects and roles are usually the ones that teach you the most, so don’t shy away from them just because they’re daunting.

About our group:

Nashville Women’s STEM group is a cross-disciplinary group of women in (or interested in) Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematical fields. We meet every other month to discuss our experiences, give advice, and give support.
