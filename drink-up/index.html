Nerdy, Brillant Women of Nashville - Drink Up!

Sep 18th, 2012

I’m bothered by how few women I know (and see at events) in the STEM community of Nashville, so I’m going to do something about it!

I’m inviting the Scientists, Engineers, Programmers, Mathematicians, every thing in between, those remotely interested in STEM, over all nerdy ladies (and those who identify as ladies) to join me for a drink Oct 26th 6:00pm at 12th South Taproom * map *.

Why only women?

I want to stop this => the leaky pipeline, encourage more women to be active in our community, and most importantly no one should feel like an outsider doing what they love most in life.

Is this a recurring event?

I hope so, I’d like this to be a monthly event. So, ladies, please come have a drink with me!
