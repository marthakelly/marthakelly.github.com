Hello Internet

May 21st, 2012

Hi, I’m Martha. I’m a developer for Cheezburger Network (I Can Has Cheezburger, Failblog, Memebase, Know Your Meme). Well, at least I will be until June. I’m really excited to announce that I’ve been accepted into the summer batch of Hacker School hosted at Etsy.

Hacker School is a three-month, full-time school in New York for becoming a better programmer. We’re free as in beer, and provide space, a little structure, time to focus, and a friendly community of smart builders dedicated to self-improvement.

I’ll spend my summer crunching code in the amazing Etsy offices in Brooklyn. Their goal is to make me “a significantly better programmer.”

My goal is to exceed their expectations.

Never graduate!
Martha
